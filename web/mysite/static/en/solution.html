Temperature Monitoring Solution
  • Place the wireless electronic temperature tags into the minimum packaging goods (medicines, vaccines, flowers, ice fresh…). Package and transport the goods with the temp tags together, to completely get a no breakpoint temperature record from the factory to the end user, it is the real sense of the temperature record of end-to-end.
  • Read the temperature file without unpacking, thus the temperature monitoring can not be faked. No need to use the dedicated device, the receivers can read the temperature information via the app on a smart phone——simple, convenient and low cost.
  • Present the temperature curve with timeline, and each temperature change during the transport can be seen at a glance. Easy to find out which step caused the over-temp and to distinguish the responsibilities of each step of whole chain.
  • Easy to get started, whatever the ice bag transport, trucking , shipping or air transport, in the existing transport modes, a single temperature tag can complete the temperature monitoring directly. It is a powerful supplement to the existing cold chain transportation system. Temperature tags can be reused, greatly reduce the cost of cold chain system investment (one-way cost less than 0.15* dollars).
Solution Features
  • A real end-to-end temperature record, no chance to falsify

Technical Specifications
  • Product name: ZenMeasure Wireless Temperature Tag
  • Product model: MOT-U201
  • Temperature range: -20℃ ~ +65℃
  • Measurement error: ±0.2℃(+20℃ ~ +50℃) ±0.5℃(-20℃ ~ +20℃, +50℃ ~ +65℃)
  • Data storage: 100,000 temperature data
  • Recording interval: 1-10 min user-selectable
  • Product dimension: 31.8 mm in diameter and 7.8 mm in height
  • Waterproof grade: IP65
  • Wireless technology: Bluetooth low energy (Smart Bluetooth)
  • Battery type: CR2025 button battery
  • Battery life (CR2025): 1year (longer usage of the electronic temperature tag can be customized)
